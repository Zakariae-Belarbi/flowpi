About FlowPI

We're a team of passionate engineering students dedicated to ensuring a seamless data flow and analyzing stock levels to provide accurate predictions with innovative technology.

Our Story

FlowPI aims to be founded in 2025 by a group of engineering students who identified a critical gap in the industrial energy monitoring market. Traditional systems were expensive, difficult to install, and offered limited insights into energy consumption patterns.

Our team set out to create an affordable, easy-to-deploy solution that leverages modern IoT technology, machine learning, real-time analytics, and Industry 4.0 principles to provide businesses with unprecedented visibility into their data flow and stock management.

This revolution is driven by a group of passionate engineering students, dedicated to providing businesses with smarter data flow management and enhanced stock analysis through innovative, Industry 4.0-inspired solutions.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with intelligent data flow management and stock analysis solutions that increase efficiency and optimize operations. We believe that better management starts with better data, and we're committed to making that data accessible, actionable, and affordable for organizations of all sizes, using the power of Raspberry Pi and Industry 4.0 principles.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every industrial facility, commercial building, and manufacturing plant has real-time visibility into their data flow and stock management, with intelligent systems automatically optimizing these processes to minimize waste and maximize efficiency. Our goal is to help improve operational efficiency across industries and empower businesses to make data-driven decisions, all through the use of Raspberry Pi and Industry 4.0 technologies.
